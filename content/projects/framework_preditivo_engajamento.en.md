## Overview

**Ongoing project.** Reported period: PIBIC 2025-2026.

This project investigates the construction of a **predictive framework for student engagement and performance** in gamified learning environments. The proposal combines **Octalysis** principles, learning-related neuroscientific indicators, learning analytics, and machine learning models to support earlier, more personalized, evidence-based pedagogical decisions.

The project builds on concrete gamification experiences in undergraduate courses, including the **Feudal Dawn** experience, and organizes their data into diagnostic instruments, indicators, and models that support the personalization of teaching strategies.

## Objectives

The main objectives are to:

- structure a framework to predict student engagement and performance in gamified learning environments;
- combine Octalysis motivational dimensions with learning, engagement, and performance indicators;
- explore machine learning models to support predictions and pedagogical recommendations;
- consolidate diagnostic instruments applicable at the start of a semester; and
- support the evolution of the {{< link-interno "/products/edutrack" "EduTrack" >}} and {{< link-interno "/products/edutrackai" "EduTrack AI" >}} products.
