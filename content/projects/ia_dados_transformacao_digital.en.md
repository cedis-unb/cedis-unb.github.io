## Overview

**Ongoing project.** Period: 2021-Present.

This project brings together research and development in **artificial intelligence**, **data analysis**, and **digital transformation**, focusing on solutions applied to academic, scientific, educational, and institutional environments. The initiative organizes a line of work that connects analytical models, machine learning, natural language processing, data curation, evidence-based decision-making, and digital process automation.

At CEDIS, this front relates to specific projects and products, including platforms for systematic reviews, scientific information analysis, academic indicators, and data-oriented digital services.

## Objectives

The main objectives are to:

- investigate AI and data analysis methods applicable to real digital transformation problems;
- develop solutions that support curation, classification, prediction, recommendation, and information analysis;
- integrate machine learning and natural language processing techniques into CEDIS digital products; and
- support institutional and academic decisions with structured, traceable, and reusable evidence.

## Related fronts

Connected fronts include:

- academic monitoring and institutional indicators, including {{< link-interno "/products/saga" "SAGA" >}}, {{< link-interno "/products/magra" "MAGRA" >}}, and {{< link-interno "/products/apoio_estudantil_fcte" "Apoio Estudantil FCTE" >}};

- automation, classification, prediction, and decision support in academic, institutional, and specialized contexts.
