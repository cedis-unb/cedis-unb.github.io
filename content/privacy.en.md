This page presents the CEDIS site privacy policy. Detailed content — including specific legal basis under the Brazilian General Data Protection Law (LGPD), retention by category, and consent mechanism — is being consolidated under issue I12 of the audit plan.

## What we currently collect

- **Aggregated navigation metrics** via Google Analytics 4 (identifier `G-ENMST48SB3`), with IP anonymization enabled. We do not collect individual identifiers nor cross-reference navigation data with other databases.
- **Language and theme preferences** (light/dark) stored locally in the user's browser via `localStorage`. This data does not leave the browser.

## Purpose

- Understand in aggregate which content is most consulted to guide editorial curation.
- Preserve the user's own preferences across visits.

## Data subject rights (LGPD, art. 18)

Data subjects may, at any time, request confirmation, access, correction, anonymization, portability, or elimination of their data, as well as information about shared use. Requests should be sent to [cedis@unb.br](mailto:cedis@unb.br).

## Contact

Data Protection Officer (UnB institutional DPO): see [https://www.gov.br/lgpd/](https://www.gov.br/lgpd/) for the University of Brasília DPO.

Site-specific contact: [cedis@unb.br](mailto:cedis@unb.br).

## Updates

This policy will be reviewed alongside issue I12 of the audit plan, when detailed sections on consent, Do Not Track (DNT) and Global Privacy Control (GPC) support, and a complete inventory of forms and external integrations will be published.
