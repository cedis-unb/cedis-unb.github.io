## {{< i18n "people_profile" >}}
- Current Position: Professor at the [University of Brasília](https://www.unb.br/) (UnB), engaged in the undergraduate [Software Engineering](http://software.unb.br/) program since 2011 and in the [Graduate Program in Applied Computing](https://ppca.unb.br/) since 2020.
- Fields of Expertise: Software Engineering, Requirements Engineering, Artificial Intelligence, team effectiveness models, human aspects in software engineering, and software engineering education.
- Institutional Role: Researcher at the Center for Software Studies, Development, and Innovation (CEDIS/UnB).

## {{< i18n "people_background" >}}
- PhD in Computer Science - Federal University of Pernambuco (UFPE), Brazil (2016-2020).
- PhD thesis: "Construction and Validation of a Software Team Effectiveness Model" (2020).
- Professional Master's in Knowledge Management and Information Technology - Catholic University of Brasília (UCB), Brazil (2005-2007).
- Specialization in Object-Oriented Systems - Catholic University of Brasília (UCB), Brazil (2002-2003).
- Bachelor’s in Data Processing - Catholic University of Brasília (UCB), Brazil (1998-2001).
- International Diploma in Coaching in Organizational Contexts from the University of London, Mexico (2013-2014).
- Certified Coach by the International School of Coaching - The Newfield Network (2006).

## {{< i18n "people_professional_activities" >}}
- Professor at UnB since 2011, contributing to undergraduate teaching in Software Engineering and, since 2020, to the Applied Computing graduate program.
- He has taught courses such as Software Requirements, Process Modeling, Software Configuration Management, and Introduction to Computer Science.
- He has also taught lato sensu graduate courses in Software Engineering at UCB, Business Management at FGV, and courses at UDF Centro Universitário.
- He has professional experience since 1999 in private organizations and in the federal government, working in software engineering projects as an analyst, project manager, and team coordinator, using RUP, agile approaches, CMMI, and MPS.Br.

## {{< i18n "people_contributions" >}}
- Registration of the Doarti software at the Brazilian National Institute of Industrial Property (INPI) (2020).
- Publication of the TPA questionnaire for assessing teamwork process antecedents in software development in the journal *Empirical Software Engineering* (2020).
- Experience in initiatives focused on digital transformation of public services, collaborative solution design, and studies on human aspects of software teams.

## {{< i18n "people_current_projects" >}}

- Improving Digital Public Service for Citizens (2023-2025).
- SUPER-R Project (2023-2024).
- Digital transformation of Brazilian government public services (2020-2023).
- HASE - Human Aspects in Software Engineering (2016-Present).
- Doarti Project: development of a technological solution to improve the donation process in the Federal District (2020-Present).
