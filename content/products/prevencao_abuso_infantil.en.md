## Product Description

**Child Sexual Abuse Prevention** is a technological product recorded in 2025 in the Lattes CV of Prof. {{< link-interno "/people/george_marsicano" "George Marsicano Corrêa" >}}. The initiative involves the development of an educational application for children, using gamification as a strategy to support prevention and communication around sensitive topics in a way suited to the target audience.

The product is connected to CEDIS work on software for social impact, combining software engineering, education, and digital experience design focused on protection and care.
