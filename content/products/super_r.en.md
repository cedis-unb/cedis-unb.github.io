## Product Description

**SUPER-R** is a set of gamified educational artifacts developed by **CEDIS/UnB** for the **Software Requirements** course of the Software Engineering program at the University of Brasília. Coordinated by Prof. {{< link-interno "/people/george_marsicano" "George Marsicano Corrêa" >}}, the product bundles a narrative, missions, a badge system, support materials, and templates to run a student-centered learning experience.

Unlike the {{< link-interno "/projects/super_r" "project page" >}}, which describes the research front and methodological evolution, this page focuses on the **artifacts delivered for classroom use**: guides, peer-review instruments, motivation indicators, and the semester mission structure.

## What the Product Offers

- Mission and operation structure aligned with the course curriculum;
- Themed narrative that anchors the experience and organizes the semester tracks;
- Peer-review instruments and motivation indicators;
- Badges and rewards grounded in the eight Core Drives of the **Octalysis** model;
- Templates, support materials, and visual artifacts reusable across other courses.

## Classroom Use

The product supports a real teaching experience:

- **plan the student's journey** across the semester with missions and challenges;
- **engage** through narrative, collaboration, and symbolic rewards;
- **assess** with peer-review instruments and continuous motivation indicators; and
- **replicate** the strategy in other courses via the shared templates.
