Brasilia, March 2026.

**Evidentia Review** is an online system developed by the **Center for Studies, Development, and Innovation in Software (CEDIS)** to support researchers in carrying out **systematic literature reviews** end to end. The platform, available at [https://evidentia.cedis.tec.br/](https://evidentia.cedis.tec.br/), was designed to help distributed teams plan, conduct, and report evidence-based reviews.

Its main features include support for defining protocols, research questions, inclusion and exclusion criteria, and extraction forms. The system also supports title/abstract and full-text screening, conflict tracking and reviewer consensus, as well as **Machine Learning** assistance for prioritization and review decision support.

A key strength of **Evidentia Review** is its focus on **transparency**, **traceability**, and **reproducibility**. The platform keeps full audit trails, provides versioned synthesis templates, and enables the generation of reproducible reports and documents, including outputs aligned with **PRISMA 2020**. In this way, the software expands CEDIS's capacity to deliver digital solutions for scientific research and evidence-based decision-making.
