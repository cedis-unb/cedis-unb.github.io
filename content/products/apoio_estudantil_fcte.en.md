## Product Description

**FCTE Student Support** is a technological product recorded in 2025 in the Lattes CV of Prof. {{< link-interno "/people/george_marsicano" "George Marsicano Corrêa" >}}. The proposal addresses the centralization of administrative information to support student development at the School of Sciences and Engineering Technologies, using a chatbot and artificial intelligence.

The product is related to digital service transformation and to improving the student experience in higher education, combining institutional information, digital service delivery, and support for academic persistence.
