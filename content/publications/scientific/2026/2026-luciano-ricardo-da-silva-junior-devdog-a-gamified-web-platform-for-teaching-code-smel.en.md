## Abstract

Paper accepted at the Tools Track of the 40th Brazilian Symposium on Software Engineering (SBES 2026), presenting DevDog, a deployed gamified web platform with theory guides, contextualized exercises, line-level smell marking, immediate feedback, collectible treats (petiscos), progressive hints, and community statistics. Implemented with React, Vite, Tailwind CSS, Express, Prisma, and PostgreSQL, covering a catalog of 19 smell types inspired by Fowler's catalog.
