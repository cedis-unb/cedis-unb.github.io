    duration: "2 semesters (TCC1 + TCC2)"
    icon: "publications"
    intro: "Write your undergraduate thesis linked to active CEDIS projects, across two semesters (TCC1 and TCC2)."
    steps:
      - "In TCC1, check the list of topics with CEDIS researchers and start scoping the problem."
      - "Schedule a talk with the advisor to align scope, feasibility, and a two-semester timeline."
      - "Formalize the supervision in the coordination system and plan deliverables with clear milestones for TCC1 and TCC2."
      - "Notable TCC2 work turns into conference papers or book chapters."

    label: "Master's — PPCA"
    audience: "Graduates in computing or related areas"
    duration: "24 months"
    icon: "publications"
    intro: "Professional Master's in Applied Computing focused on applied research and technological output."
    steps:
      - "Check the annual PPCA/UnB call (usually open in the second semester)."
      - "Identify a CEDIS supervisor with aligned research and talk before applying."
      - "Prepare the pre-project observing registered research lines and openings per supervisor."
      - "Once accepted, you enter a 24-month program with applied dissertation and technical artifact."

    intro: "PhD in Applied Computing — scientific depth with applied impact."
