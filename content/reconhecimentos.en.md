CEDIS's work is recognized in national and international forums for the quality of its research, for teaching innovation, and for the impact of its products and projects. This page consolidates the main recognitions received by the center and its researchers.

## 2025

### Honorable Mention — UnB Annual Award for Innovation in Undergraduate Teaching (DEG/UnB)

The **Alvorecer Feudal** project by Prof. {{< link-interno "/people/sergio_freitas" "Sergio Freitas" >}} received an Honorable Mention at the UnB Annual Award for Innovation in Undergraduate Teaching, in the "Use of technological resources and production of innovative teaching materials" category. [Read the full coverage]({{< relref "/posts/news-post.019" >}}).

## 2024

### Best Paper — IEEE ITHET 2024 (Paris)

A paper presented at the IEEE International Conference on Information Technology Based Higher Education and Training (ITHET) received the best paper award. [Read the full coverage]({{< relref "/posts/news-post.013" >}}).

### Best Paper — ICCSA 2024 (Hanoi, Vietnam)

The **Canal Ciência** project, in partnership with IBICT, was awarded best paper at the 24th International Conference on Computational Science and Its Applications. [Read the full coverage]({{< relref "/posts/news-post.010" >}}).

## 2023

### Honorable Mention — Learning Indicators

CEDIS professors received an honorable mention in teaching innovation for their work on Learning Indicators, which also resulted in the publication of the book of the same name. [Read the full coverage]({{< relref "/posts/news-post.002" >}}).
