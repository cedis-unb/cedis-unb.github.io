Brasilia, March 9, 2026  

**CEDIS** presents **Evidentia Review**, a software platform developed by the center to support researchers and distributed teams in planning, conducting, and reporting **systematic literature reviews (SLRs)**. The platform is available at [https://evidentia.cedis.tec.br/](https://evidentia.cedis.tec.br/).  

The system brings together tools to structure protocols, organize inclusion and exclusion criteria, run title/abstract and full-text screening, register reviewer conflicts, and support decisions with **Machine Learning** resources. Evidentia also maintains full audit trails, strengthening transparency and traceability throughout the review process.  

Another highlight is the generation of reproducible reports and documents, including outputs aligned with **PRISMA 2020**, along with versioned synthesis templates and extraction auditing. With Evidentia Review, CEDIS expands its portfolio of digital solutions focused on evidence-based research.  

Evidentia Review now also joins the **CEDIS digital products** showcase.
