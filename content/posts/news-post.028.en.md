Brasília, July 10, 2026

**The paper _A Motivation-Aware Diagnostic Instrument for Personalized and Gamified Computing Education_ has been accepted at the XXXVII Brazilian Symposium on Computers in Education (SBIE 2026, submission #27258).**

The paper is authored by Prof. {{< link-interno "/people/sergio_freitas" "Sergio Freitas" >}}, Daniel Rodrigues da Rocha, Davi Rodrigues da Rocha, and Mylena Angélica Silva Farias, all from CEDIS, and describes a semester-start diagnostic instrument for personalized and gamified computing education. The instrument was evaluated across three undergraduate courses — Human-Computer Interaction, Compilers, and Introduction to Gamification — gathering 245 valid responses with good internal consistency (α = 0.880).

This diagnostic instrument underpins **EduTrack**, a platform for tracking, analyzing, and gamifying classes, and **EduTrack AI**, an AI API for academic performance prediction in gamified environments, both developed by Daniel, Davi, and Mylena as part of their undergraduate research at CEDIS.

The products are available at [edutrack.cedis.tec.br](https://edutrack.cedis.tec.br/) and [ai.edutrack.cedis.tec.br](https://ai.edutrack.cedis.tec.br/), and can be explored further on the {{< link-interno "/products/edutrack" "EduTrack" >}} and {{< link-interno "/products/edutrackai" "EduTrack AI" >}} pages at CEDIS.
