CEDIS maintains reference materials for public communication of research, institutional collaboration, and consultation by people interested in Software Engineering, Artificial Intelligence, gamification, and digital transformation of public services.

## Institutional contact

Requests for institutional information, event schedules, high-resolution images, or data about specific projects can be sent to:

- **E-mail:** [cedis@unb.br](mailto:cedis@unb.br)
- **Coordination:** {{< link-interno "/people/sergio_freitas" "Prof. Sergio Freitas" >}}

We respond preferentially during business hours (Brasília, UTC−3). For time-sensitive requests, please include the context and deadline in the e-mail subject.

## Expertise areas for consultation

CEDIS brings together researchers who can contribute to technical consultations, events, outreach materials, and partnerships on:

- Software Engineering (architecture, requirements, quality, testing)
- Artificial Intelligence and Natural Language Processing
- Learning Analytics and gamification in education
- Digital transformation of public services
- Cybersecurity
- High-performance computing

See the [Areas of expertise]({{< relref "/categories/knowledge_areas" >}}) page for a full map.
