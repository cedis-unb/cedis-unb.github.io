Undergraduate research is the most common entry path for undergraduate students who want to join CEDIS research activities.

## How to start

1. Read CEDIS's research areas and choose two or three that match your interest.
2. Find the responsible researchers and send a short email explaining your interest.
3. Follow PIBIC/PIBIT calls and voluntary openings throughout the year.
4. If selected, join meetings, write a report, and present your results at the undergraduate research congress.
