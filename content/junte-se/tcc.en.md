Software Engineering students can develop undergraduate theses linked to CEDIS projects, products, and research lines.

## How to start

1. In TCC1, check topics with CEDIS researchers and scope a feasible problem.
2. Talk to the advisor to align scope, timeline, and deliverables for TCC1 and TCC2.
3. Formalize the supervision with the course coordination.
4. Strong works can evolve into papers, tools, or book chapters.
