Institutional partnerships turn real problems into research, development, and innovation projects.

## How to start

1. Describe the problem, audience, and expected outcome.
2. Indicate timelines, constraints, and funding sources when available.
3. Contact the coordination or a researcher aligned with the topic.
4. Formalization may involve an agreement, work plan, and specific governance.
