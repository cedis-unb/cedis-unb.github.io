Outreach connects research, development, and real social demands.

## How to start

1. Follow open opportunities.
2. Choose a project or product aligned with your profile.
3. Talk to the responsible researcher about availability, responsibilities, and deliverables.
4. When applicable, participation may generate an institutional declaration or record.
