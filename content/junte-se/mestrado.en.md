The master's path is appropriate for people seeking applied research, technological output, and methodological depth.

## How to start

1. Follow the annual PPCA/UnB call.
2. Identify a CEDIS researcher aligned with your pre-project.
3. Talk before applying to evaluate fit and feasibility.
4. Prepare a clear proposal with problem, method, timeline, and expected artifact.
