New ideas can become projects when there is thematic fit, available team capacity, and feasible scope.

## How to start

1. Write a short summary of the problem and the audience that benefits from the project.
2. Explain why CEDIS is a good partner for the project.
3. List known data, systems, resources, or constraints.
4. Send the initial proposal for fit assessment.
