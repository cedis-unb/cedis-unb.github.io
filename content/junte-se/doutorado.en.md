The PhD path is designed for deeper research with clear scientific contribution and applied impact.

## How to start

1. Study CEDIS groups, projects, and recent publications.
2. Send a first proposal, CV, and timeline to a potential advisor.
3. If aligned, formalize your application through the PPCA/UnB call.
4. Plan a publication and collaboration strategy from the beginning.
