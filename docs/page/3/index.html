Our mission

Research, development, and education in software at the University of Brasilia.

At the forefront of innovation since 2013, the CEDIS from the University of Brasília is dedicated to shaping the future of Software Engineering, Learning, and Artificial Intelligence. We bring together students, researchers, and professionals on a collaborative journey of discovery, development, and application of transformative technologies. Our commitment is to lead by example, promoting sustainable solutions and empowering the next generations of innovators.
