Academic and Professional Profile

  • Current Position: Adjunct Professor at the University of Brasília (UnB), engaged in the undergraduate Software Engineering program since 2022.
  • Fields of Expertise: Parallel computing , high-performance computing , graphics processing units (GPU), cloud computing, distributed systems, and bioinformatics.
  • Institutional Role: Participates in research and development projects with IBICT focused on open informational infrastructure, data lakes, and scientific information retrieval.

Professional Activities and Teaching

  • Higher education professor at the University of Brasília since 2022, working in the Software Engineering program.
  • He has taught courses such as Fundamentals of Operating Systems and Algorithms and Computer Programming.
  • He was also a professor at the Federal Institute of Brasília (2018-2022), including serving as coordinator of the Internet Systems Technology program.
  • He has previous industry experience as a senior developer and project manager at Aker Security Solutions, working with security software, Linux, networking, OpenSSL, and product maintenance.
  • He develops research in high-performance computing, cloud computing, distributed systems, and bioinformatics, applying these areas to solve complex problems.

Contributions and Acknowledgments

  • Honorable Mention in the Thesis and Dissertations Contest in Computer Architectures and High-Performance Computing (WSCAD-CTD), Brazilian Computer Society (2018).
  • Best Achievement Awards - PUMPS 2015, Universitat Politècnica de Catalunya.
  • Reviewer for journals such as Concurrency and Computation: Practice & Experience, Journal of the ACM, and Parallel Computing.
  • Development of tools and software such as CUDA-Sankoff, Foldalign 2.5, and PA-Star for bioinformatics and high-performance processing.
